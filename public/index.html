Understand Your Prakriti

In Ayurveda, every individual is born with a unique combination of the three doshas. Understanding your Prakriti is the foundation of personalised wellness.

Vata — Air & Space

Creative, energetic and quick-thinking. Vata types benefit from grounding routines and warm, nourishing foods.

Vata Dosha

Pitta — Fire & Water

Focused, ambitious and intense. Pitta types thrive with cooling practices and a balanced, moderate lifestyle.

Pitta Dosha

Kapha — Earth & Water

Calm, steady and nurturing. Kapha types benefit from stimulating activities and light, energising foods.
